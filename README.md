## üîÑ **NABL-COMPLIANT WORKFLOW**

### **üìã Complete Lab Operations Lifecycle**

#### **Phase 1: Patient Registration & Visit Creation**
1. **Reception** ‚Üí Create visit with comprehensive patient details
2. **Visit Status**: `PENDING` ‚Üí Ready for sample collection

#### **Phase 2: Sample Collection (NABL Compliant)**
1. **Phlebotomy** ‚Üí Collect samples with proper documentation
2. **Chain of Custody** ‚Üí Record collection conditions and personnel
3. **Sample Status**: `COLLECTED` ‚Üí `IN_TRANSIT` ‚Üí `RECEIVED`
4. **Visit Status**: `PENDING` ‚Üí `IN_PROGRESS`

#### **Phase 3: Laboratory Processing**
1. **Sample Accessioning** ‚Üí Assign sample numbers and barcodes
2. **Test Assignment** ‚Üí Add tests based on physician orders
3. **Analysis** ‚Üí Perform laboratory tests and enter results
4. **Sample Status**: `RECEIVED` ‚Üí `PROCESSING` ‚Üí `ANALYSIS_COMPLETE`

#### **Phase 4: Quality Control & Approval**
1. **Result Review** ‚Üí Technical review of test results
2. **Quality Validation** ‚Üí Ensure NABL compliance
3. **Supervisor Approval** ‚Üí Pathologist approval of results
4. **Visit Status**: `IN_PROGRESS` ‚Üí `AWAITING_APPROVAL` ‚Üí `APPROVED`

#### **Phase 5: Report Generation (ULR System)**
1. **ULR Number Generation** ‚Üí Unique Laboratory Report numbering
2. **PDF Report Creation** ‚Üí NABL-compliant reports with barcodes
3. **Report Authorization** ‚Üí Final authorization by chief pathologist
4. **Report Status**: `DRAFT` ‚Üí `GENERATED` ‚Üí `AUTHORIZED`

#### **Phase 6: Billing & Payment**
1. **Bill Generation** ‚Üí Automated billing based on completed tests
2. **Payment Processing** ‚Üí Record payment and method
3. **Visit Status**: `APPROVED` ‚Üí `BILLED` ‚Üí `COMPLETED`

#### **Phase 7: Sample Disposal (NABL Requirement)**
1. **Sample Storage** ‚Üí Proper storage conditions maintained
2. **Disposal Documentation** ‚Üí Record disposal method and batch
3. **Sample Status**: `STORED` ‚Üí `DISPOSED`
